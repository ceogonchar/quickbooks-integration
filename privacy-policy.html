Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 1, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Gonchar Group LLC ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our QuickBooks integration application ("the App").

2. Information We Collect

When you connect your QuickBooks account to our App, we may access:

  • Company financial data (transactions, accounts, reports)
  • Account information (company name, contact details)
  • Banking and payment information as stored in QuickBooks

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information accessed through QuickBooks solely to:

  • Provide financial analysis and reporting
  • Categorize and organize transactions
  • Generate insights and recommendations
  • Automate bookkeeping tasks as directed by you

4. Data Storage and Security

Your QuickBooks data is accessed in real-time through secure API connections. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. Access tokens are encrypted and stored securely.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your financial data with third parties. Your data is used exclusively for providing services to you.

6. Your Rights

You may disconnect the App from your QuickBooks account at any time through QuickBooks settings. Upon disconnection, we will no longer have access to your QuickBooks data.
